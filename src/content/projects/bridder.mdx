## Why this project

Building products taught me to ask harder questions about the data underneath
them. Bridder was where that lesson landed.

## What I did

- Designed and built the product end to end — frontend, data model, and the
  matching logic that connected both sides of the marketplace.
- Made deliberate choices about what data to collect and what to leave out.
- Shipped to real users and iterated on the gap between the demo and reality.

## Outcome

Bridder shipped — but the more durable outcome was the shift in how I think.
The interface was the easy part; the data model was where trust, fairness, and
correctness actually got decided.
